title: System, Apparatus and Method for Pedal Control
description:  US Patent #10,180,699 by NASA's Johnson Space Center describes a flight control system utilizing two sets of pedals and motion tracking sensors for controlling movement of an object in three-dimensional space. The prototype integrates linear and rotary encoders, an aluminum/steel structure, and embedded computing to track and translate the pilot's foot movements into real-time control inputs, allowing full 6 DOF navigation using only the feet. The system is housed within a portable weatherproof case with a removable pedal assembly for deployment. Specific design and implementation details are protected under NDA.
