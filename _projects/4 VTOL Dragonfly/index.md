title: VTOL Dragonfly
description:  The Dragonfly is a domestically designed and manufactured VTOL UAV developed to demonstrate the benefits of tilt-rotor and variable wing technology in smaller drones, with a focus on improvements in range, speed, and payload capacity. The UAV integrates a modular payload bay, onboard machine learning camera, and embedded computing for autonomous target identification and navigation, alongside a tilt-rotor thrust system enabling both vertical takeoff and efficient forward flight. Design process features retractable wings, tilt-rotor pusher propeller, and an automated payload bay.
