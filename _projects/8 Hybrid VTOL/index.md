title: Hybrid VTOL
description:  The hybrid VTOL aircraft is a small, lightweight prototype developed as a proof of concept for a fuel-efficient VTOL operable in space-constrained environments, such as aboard ships. The prototype features 5 internal ducted propellers, 4 of which are electrically powered fans providing primary lift during vertical takeoff and landing, and a central gasoline-driven fan for efficient forward flight. An onboard generator driven by the engine recharges the battery mid-flight, enabling repeated transitions between electric vertical flight and combustion-powered forward flight. The design incorporates a V-tail to minimize weight and upward-folding wings to reduce the aircraft's footprint for storage.
