title: Counter Rotating Coaxial IBC System
description:  The Counter Rotating Coaxial IBC System is a demonstrative technology modeled after NASA's Individual Blade Control (IBC) Project. The purpose of the project is to explore the feasibility and benefits of utilizing IBC in new helicopter designs, such a dual counter-rotating coaxial propellers. The system integrates 8 precision actuators, a large rotary motor, and a counter-rotating gearbox. The design is validated through FEA and CFD before being manufactured, which is then tested using SW-420 vibration sensors and a wind tunnel. Design process features solar energy driven motors, multiple gearbox variations, and a pusher propeller to replace conventional tail torque rotors.
