title: BlendBot Ink Mixer
description:  The BlendBot Ink Mixer is an assistive technology device developed in partnership with Spectrum Designs to improve the plastisol ink mixing process for neurodiverse workers. The device integrates a high-torque motor, custom planetary gear system, and three interchangeable whisking attachments mounted to an aluminum lid, allowing it to clamp onto any standard 5-gallon bucket for safe and thorough ink mixing. A handheld controller with a rotary encoder and LCD display gives users real-time control over mixing speed and direction, while a dead-man switch ensures the device only operates under active supervision. The system is powered by a standard 18V Milwaukee battery, keeping it compatible with tools already used at the facility.
